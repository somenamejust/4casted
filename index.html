FAQ

What is 4casted?
−
4casted is a prediction markets aggregator. It brings markets from multiple platforms into one interface, so you can compare prices, spot mismatches, and track opportunities without jumping between apps.
Why use 4casted instead of native platforms?
+
Native platforms show only their own markets. 4casted shows overlapping markets across platforms in one view. You see price gaps, liquidity differences, and arbitrage as they form. Forget tab switching, blind spots, or multi-account mess. We also provide structured market intelligence and incentives, such as trading rebates, that native platforms don't offer.
What will I get with a premium subscription?
+
4casted Premium gives you deeper visibility into how markets overlap and diverge across platforms. Instead of manually scanning markets, Premium users see significantly more matched markets, price discrepancies, and emerging arbitrage signals in real time.
Is this live data or delayed?
+
Live. We ingest real-time price and liquidity updates via APIs and WebSocket streams. Refresh speed depends on the source platform, but updates are processed continuously to surface discrepancies as they happen.
What is Smart Routing, and when will it be available?
+
Smart Routing is our execution layer. It will automatically route orders to the best venue based on price and liquidity. 4casted Smart Routing will roll out in a later phase once the execution infrastructure is fully deployed.
